Give Someone Their

Water Day

More than 780 million people around the world lack access to safe water. This means millions of women cannot contribute to the prosperity of their communities because their time is spent finding water; their children are not going to school because they too must collect water; and a child is dying every 21 seconds due to a water-related disease.

In honor of Water Day 2014 we're celebrating one of the best days of someone's life – the day they get access to safe water.

For $25.00 you can give someone their water day.
